The Celebration of Haji

In the season of Haji , the school celebrated by the children perfromance of the manask , all children were dressed in white and rotating around a Ka'ba , This was under the supervision of the school headmaster Mr. Nasser Salama and variety of teachers , it was preformed by second and third grade students , and they were taught the manask in a very fun ways.
